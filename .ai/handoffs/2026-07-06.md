## Decisions

- **Classic PAT over fine-grained**: Fine-grained PATs require repo ownership to scope. Classic PAT with `repo` scope works for collaborator repos. Decision made by constraint.
- **Project-root .env over ~/.hermes/.env**: Per-project .env is self-documenting via .env.example, standard pattern, scoped naturally. User preference.
- **AGENTS.md at root over .ai/**: Root placement gets auto-detection by Hermes and other AI tools.
- **Env vars over repo-level git config**: `GIT_AUTHOR_*` and `GIT_COMMITTER_*` env vars scoped to the Hermes process, Anna's global config unaffected. No accidental misattribution.
- **Merge tiers over universal human gate**: Tier 1 auto-merge for small docs changes, tier 3 manual for large/cross-cutting. Errs on caution.
- **Threaded review conversations**: All review comments threaded for human readability mid-loop.
