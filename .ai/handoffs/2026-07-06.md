- The Chief Engineer must NOT invent gameplay. All gameplay rules are specified in the docs above. If something is missing, stop and route back to the Creative Director / Lead Systems Designer.
- The MVP uses a **single** resource (wood). Do not introduce stone, food, or other resources.
- The town hall upgrade costs use a 3× multiplier per tier. Ensure costs match the table in Buildings.md.
- Forester's hut auto-plants trees — no villager assignment needed.
- Villagers do NOT walk back after gathering. They idle at the node.
- No camera panning, zoom, or scrolling in the MVP.
- No demolish/relocate in the MVP.
- No decorations, roads, or districts in the MVP.
