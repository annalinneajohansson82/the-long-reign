# Purpose

Define the event system — what types of events occur in the game, how they are triggered, and how they affect the kingdom and its history.

---

# Overview

Events are occurrences that happen during gameplay — some scripted, some emergent from the simulation, and some tied to the passage of seasons. Events are recorded in the Chronicle and, for important individuals, in their Living Memories biography. The best stories in The Long Reign come from simulation rather than scripted events, but both types exist.

Events span the full range of kingdom life: from seasonal harvests to hero deaths, from colony foundings to disasters.

---

# Design Goals

- **Emergent Stories.** The best events emerge naturally from the simulation — births, marriages, discoveries — rather than from scripted triggers.
- **Living World.** Seasons, villager lifecycles, and colony development all generate events that make the kingdom feel alive.
- **Historical Record.** Events are captured in the Chronicle and Living Memories, creating a unique history for each save.
- **Natural Consequences.** Negative events (revolt, fires, decline) emerge from poor management rather than arbitrary triggers.

---

# Non-Goals

- Events are not a quest log or objective tracker.
- The game does not feature daily quests or rotating event schedules.
- This document does not define specific event probabilities, cooldowns, or trigger conditions.

# Chronicle Integration

All significant events are automatically recorded in the Chronicle. The Chronicle is one of the game's flagship systems. Recorded event types include:
- Births
- Marriages
- Deaths
- Colony founding
- Discoveries
- Disasters
- Hero achievements
- Completed wonders
- Wars
- Technological advances

See `03-simulation/Chronicle.md` for the full specification.

# Living Memories Integration

Important people (heroes and significant villagers) have events recorded in their personal biographies. Examples of biography data:
- Birthplace
- Profession
- Achievements
- Family
- Expeditions
- Legacy

See `03-simulation/Living Memories.md` for the full specification.

# Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Lifecycle events (births, marriages, deaths) are generated by the villager simulation and recorded in the Chronicle.
- [ ] Seasons trigger relevant gameplay events (harvests, winter hardships).
- [ ] Hero events (achievements, recruitment, death/injury) are tracked.
- [ ] Disaster events (revolt, abandonment, fires, decline) can occur as consequences of poor management.
- [ ] Significant events appear in relevant Living Memories biographies.
- [ ] Events are recorded with timestamps or in-game dates in the Chronicle.
